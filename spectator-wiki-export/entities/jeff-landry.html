Jeff Landry

Associate Vice President for Student Affairs at Hamilton College and Chair of the Hamilton Emergency Response Team (HERT) during the COVID-19 pandemic. Landry served as the college’s primary public spokesperson on pandemic response in spring 2020, reporting on Hamilton’s adherence to CDC and New York State Department of Health guidance. He addressed the Student Assembly on March 10, 2020, outlining quarantine protocols and communication plans, and was quoted in the Spectator’s March 12, 2020 pandemic coverage stating the college would “figure out a way to do it and help students out, make sure that students stay healthy.”

Key Appearances

  • Presented COVID-19 guidance at the March 10, 2020 Student Assembly meeting (Spectator, March 12, 2020)
